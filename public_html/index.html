Welcome to YouSync !



The purpose of this system is to sync youtube videos with your peers without the need to count down on voice chat.
  • Start by identifying yourself by typing "setname (your desired username)" otherwise a random generated name will be used

  • You can just join a room or create one by typing "room (room name) (room password)"

  • To cue a video type "play (video URL)"

  • To access the video cue type "list"

  • To remove a video from the list type "remove (video index on list)"

  • To skip a video type "skip"

  • To exit a room type "exit"

Every basic control on the player should sync like pause, play and seek (i hope) but audio volume won't sync so your hears dont get blasted with earrape songs.
